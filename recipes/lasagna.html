Lasagna



Lasagna is very tasty. Lasagna consists of alternating layers of sauce, noodles, bechamel, and cheese. It can also be made with vegetables like eggplants.


Ingredients:

  • 1 box of Lasagna noodles
  • 28 oz can of tomato
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Basil to taste
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Make the bechamel
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
