Apple Pie



Apple Pie is a great dessert to make in the fall. Apples are in season and its warm spices really bring a smile to your face.


Ingredients:

  • Honeycrip Apples
  • Granny Smith Apples
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Lemon

Steps:

  1. Make the pie crust
  2. Make the apple pie filling
  3. Add the filling to the crust
  4. Add the top crust
  5. Bake the pie
